Cat's Frozen Addon — FrozenMonsters sprite fold (Project Brutality 2022)
=======================================================================

Source (upstream): Cat's Frozen Addon V2.6.2 — `Sprites/Monsters/FrozenMonsters/`
(SchrödingCat; see README.txt in that package for full credits.)

These notes were originally placed at SPRITES/Monsters/CatsFrozenFrozen/ASSET_NOTES.txt
but UZDoom 4.14.3 attempts to load every file under SPRITES/ as a texture and emits
"Invalid data encountered for texture .../asset_notes.txt" at startup. The notes were
relocated here (alongside the related DECORATE / ZScript) to silence that warning.

Layout in THIS repo
-------------------
Sprites remain under per-monster subfolders here so short lump names (IFZD*, BFZD*,
MFZD*, CFZD*, PFZD*, RFZD*, CYFZ*, SPFZ*) stay grouped and do not collide with Project
Brutality stock sprites elsewhere under SPRITES/. Files are a mix of `.png`, `.lmp`,
and extensionless 8.3 lumps — copied verbatim from upstream where possible. The actual
sprite directory is SPRITES/Monsters/CatsFrozenFrozen/.

Usage
-----
Referenced only by ZScript classes in `zscript/Monsters/CatsFrozen/PB_FrozenSolid.zc`
and spawned via `PB_FrozenSolidHandler` on Ice / Freeze / Frost / IceBeam kills (always on).
