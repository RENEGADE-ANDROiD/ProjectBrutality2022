- **PDA cursor: aspect-aware sizing for high-res `ZCURSOR.png` art.** The PDA mouse-cursor draw in **`PB_PDAWeaponContainer.RenderOverlay`** previously hard-coded `pdaCursorSize = 32.0 * pdaCursorScale` for both width and height, on the assumption that **`GRAPHICS/Weapon Wheel/ZCURSOR.png`** was 32×32 (the old × cursor).
