PROJECT BRUTALITY 2022 — CREDITS
================================

This file lists people, projects, and codebases that Project Brutality 2022
builds on or includes. A short index is first; the section after the rule line
reproduces the historical DetailedCredits material line-by-line for reference.

Attribution is best-effort across a very large mod. Sources include in-repo
headers and comments, SNDINFO / GLDEFS / TEXTURES notes, and DetailedCredits.txt.
Corrections belong in project discussion channels.

Engine and base projects
- UZDoom / GZDoom and the wider ZDoom family tooling.
- Project Brutality: https://github.com/pa1nki113r/Project_Brutality
- Brutal Doom and contributors (including Sergeant_Mark_IV).

Maintainers (this distribution)
- RENEGADE ANDROID
- doc

Gameplay and content in this release
- DarkShadow’s Monster Sounds Overhaul: monster SFX (based on Ravik’s Soundpack, with
  content from multiple Doom games) — integrated by replacing matching files under
  SOUNDS/ and by extending a small set of caco/imp SNDINFO pools to use additional
  variants.
- Universal Kill Streaks and Power-Up Rewards: based on the Universal Kill
  Streaks and PowerUp Rewards add-on, adapted to this project’s names, menus,
  and message paths.
- “Unless You Have Powah!” reward content: used in the reward and power-up
  pools for this build.
- UT announcer: kill-streak announcer audio, menu routing, and volume
  handling wired to the project cvars and options.
- Metal Sniper, XM-21, ShieldSaw, Molotov: weapon packages included in the
  supported weapon and equipment set (see per-file notes). Shield Saw behavior
  traces to **GK_ShieldSaw** (**HUNG**).
- Commander Shotgun (Combat Shotgun, CSSG, `PB_CSSG`): ZScript stack — special
  wheel, magazine flow, upgrade tokens, projectiles — on `PB_WeaponBase`, with
  spawners, status bar, spawn toggles, and test utilities. RENEGADE ANDROID, doc,
  and repository contributors.
- Excavator (UAC-M2) launcher: based on the PBX-Weapons / Project Brutality
  X Excavator (ThePopeofDope, BeefRice; ZScript on `PB_WeaponBase` in this
  build). Special thanks in upstream: Nemesis_Vampy, Popguy12, Jaih1r0.
- Project Brutality Weapons Pack (weapons in this build include): Hell
  Pistoler, Bio Acid Launcher, Mancubus Flame Cannon, Demon
  Tech Minigun, Argent Sith, Beam Katana, **Pro-Surv Ballista**
  (The Pope of Dope), with supporting projectiles and tokens. Equipment: Freeze Nade, Hook, Void Grenade,
  Freezebot, ElecPod. Naming, spawners, menu toggles, and HUD: RENEGADE ANDROID
  and doc.
- Credit to BeefRice and **Jaih1r0** for PBX HUD elements and many weapon improvements and systems.
- **Shield Saw** (GK_ShieldSaw lineage): **HUNG** — folded as quick-melee **`ShieldSaw_token`** + recall; see **`AGENTS.md`**.
- Rocket jump, plasma wall climb, and instant weapon switch: included as
  configurable options with PB cvar names.

Monsters and packs
- **Project Survival** monster additions (fodder zombies + passive hazards):
  **The Pope of Dope (ThePopeOfDope)**. Folded under
  `actors/Monsters/T1-Grunts/ProjectSurvivalFodder/` and
  `actors/Monsters/T1-Grunts/ProjectSurvivalPassive/` plus
  `zscript/Monsters/ProjectSurvival/` and `zscript/Spawner/PB_ProjectSurvival*`.
  Includes Shambler, Burster, Blighter, Screamer, Spiker, Puker, CyberFodder
  (fodder set) and Pustule, Mimic, Tentacle, TritePod / TritePodCeiling, Hangman,
  Trite (passive set) — sprites, sounds, monster behavior, and original
  spawner / passive-pod coding by The Pope of Dope; integrated as opt-in
  reinforcement spawns alongside vanilla zombies in this build.
- Glory Kills: lineage from D4D / Brutal Doom Black Edition
  (SNDINFO.GloryKills and related). **Glory Kill Eternal HUD** PNGs live under
  **`GRAPHICS/HUD/glorykills/newhud/`**; **equipment launcher** first-person overlays
  (**Flame Belch** / **Ice Bomb**) use **`SPRITES/WEAPONS/GloryKillLauncher/ShoulderCannon/`** (**`SCL0`/`SCF0`**
  and **`LCL0`/`LCF0`**) updated from the **Project Brutality PB_Staging** Glory Kills
  line (shoulder placement: flame right, ice left).
- Monster Pack: content line documented with SNDINFO.MonsterPack and tier files.
- Brutal Doom *El Diablo* Edition: additional first-person and expanded execution /
  finisher sprite sets (`SPRITES/MONSTERS/fatalitys/eld_eld_*` and related) merged
  into the Glory Kill / `NEWPLAYE` player state flow (not a full TC load).
- Project Brutality Legacy: execution / stagger “handoff” behaviour (`GoExecution`,
  `Death.ExeCution`… routing) and related audio folded from Legacy-line trees
  into core `PB_` monster DECORATE in this build.
- Brutal Pack (e.g. V10-line community packs): selective finisher, weapon, or
  VFX *fragments* adapted during porting; this distribution does not ship
  Brutal Pack as a complete add-on. Honour original Brutal Pack / author terms
  if reusing or redistributing extracted work.
- Realm667 and other in-file notes (e.g. Overlord GLDEFS: Eriance, Dreadopp,
  Ghastly Dragon, Keksdose, MagicWazard; Cyber Baron — Ben2k9/Virtue; Ice Vile
  — realm667 + PA1NKI113R; many more in the detailed list below). Realm667 is
  the long-running *community resource* hub; this mod uses many of its
  free-to-use or credited bases across monsters and props.

Gore: optional Brutal Doom 22 (BDv22) add-on
- **Brutal Doom 22** — optional, opt-in gore: blood mist, corpse guts, flying
  meat, organs / small-brain style props, limb subset, and a PB/BDv22 gore
  *mix* cvar. Implemented with separate `BDv22_*` class names, `zscript/Gore/`
  handlers (`BDv22GoreHandler`, `BDv22GoreMergeHandler`, `BDv22GoreMist`), and
  assets under `SPRITES/GORE/BDv22/` and `lumps/includes/bdv22_modeldef.inc` (included from `modeldef.txt`) where used. Credited to
  the **Brutal Doom 22** project and its artists; that content remains third-party
  — keep upstream credits and any distribution limits the BDv22 project requires.
  Does *not* replace core Project Brutality or Nash gore; it layers on when enabled
  in **Options → Brutality 2022 Additions** (or the matching entry under
  **Gore/Debris** in visual settings).
